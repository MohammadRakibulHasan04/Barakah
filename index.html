Mango Varieties

Alphonso (Hapus)

The king of mangoes, known for its rich flavor, smooth texture, and vibrant saffron color.

Kesar

Famous for its bright orange pulp and sweet, aromatic taste. A favorite for juices and sweets.

Langra

Juicy and tangy, with a unique flavor profile. Green even when ripe and loved for its distinct taste.

Dasheri

Sweet, aromatic, and has a long, slender shape. Popular for its smooth, fiberless flesh.

Badami

Often called the Alphonso of Karnataka. Juicy, sweet, and has a delightful aroma.
